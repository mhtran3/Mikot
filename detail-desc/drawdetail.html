OR/ange
DATE:     may 2021 CATEGORY: animation MEDIUM:   digital (procreate, adobe cc) PURPOSE:  school project

OR/ange explores a futuristic world in which the orange, a once-delicious but now highly-toxic fruit, has been repurposed as the latest source of clean energy. We follow a rookie delivery boy as he travels across the city to help power up various businesses in need, but we quickly discover that not everything is as it seems.
